## Executive Summary

This analysis focuses on the **41,339 unpopular names** (99.44% of all names) that averaged fewer than 500 babies per year. These represent the vast diversity of naming choices, from extremely rare names given to just a handful of babies to names on the rise that haven't yet reached mainstream popularity.

**Key Finding:** The unpopular name landscape is dominated by extremely rare names (67%), but contains hidden gems - rising names that could become tomorrow's popular choices.

## Unpopular Names: 8 Distinct Archetypes

### 1. **Extremely Rare - Declining** (Cluster 0)
- **Count:** 15,109 names (36.5% of unpopular, 36.3% of all)
- **Characteristics:**
  - Peak: ~8 babies per year (very low)
  - Slight negative trajectory (-0.24)
  - Early mean (3.3) > Recent mean (0.9)
  - These names are fading into obscurity
- **Examples:** Kirstie, Becky, Bethanie
- **Pattern:** Names that were never popular and are now used even less

### 2. **Extremely Rare - Stable** (Cluster 1)
- **Count:** 12,613 names (30.5% of unpopular, 30.3% of all)
- **Characteristics:**
  - Peak: ~4 babies per year (extremely low)
  - Near-zero trajectory (+0.36)
  - Virtually no early presence (0.004)
  - Consistently given to just a few babies
- **Examples:** Waziha, Sidak, Elhan
- **Pattern:** Rare cultural, ethnic, or unique spellings that persist at very low levels

### 3. **Emerging Rare Names** (Cluster 5)
- **Count:** 12,171 names (29.4% of unpopular)
- **Characteristics:**
  - Peak: ~16 babies per year
  - Positive trajectory (+3.55)
  - Recent mean (7.9) > Early mean (1.6)
  - Growing but still very rare
- **Examples:** Adonis, Maison, Hailey (variant spellings)
- **Pattern:** New names or spellings gaining traction but not yet mainstream

### 4. **Rising Uncommon** (Cluster 4)
- **Count:** 1,040 names (2.5% of unpopular)
- **Characteristics:**
  - Peak: ~166 babies per year
  - Strong trajectory (+13.40)
  - Recent mean (89) >> Early mean (42)
  - On the path to popularity
- **Examples:** Mckenzie, Mackenzie, Milly
- **Pattern:** Alternative spellings and emerging trends with momentum

### 5. **Rapid Riser** (Cluster 7)
- **Count:** 195 names (0.47% of unpopular)
- **Characteristics:**
  - Peak: ~524 babies per year (approaching popular threshold!)
  - Strong trajectory (+15.07)
  - Recent mean (362) >>> Early mean (93)
  - Could break into "popular" soon
- **Examples:** Dexter, Ashton, Faith
- **Pattern:** Names accelerating toward mainstream acceptance

### 6. **Rising Star** (Cluster 6)
- **Count:** 32 names (0.08% of unpopular)
- **Characteristics:**
  - Peak: ~1,203 babies per year (would be moderate classics if â‰¥500 avg)
  - **Explosive trajectory (+58.78)**
  - Recent mean (888) >>> Early mean (38)
  - Most likely to become popular next
- **Examples:** Keira, Bonnie, Lacey
- **Pattern:** Dramatic growth stories - these are tomorrow's popular names

### 7. **Declining Uncommon** (Cluster 2)
- **Count:** 145 names (0.35% of unpopular)
- **Characteristics:**
  - Peak: ~471 babies per year
  - Negative trajectory (-0.71)
  - Early mean (338) >>> Recent mean (64)
  - Names that fell from near-popularity

### 1. The Dominance of Rarity
